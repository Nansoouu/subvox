## Token system

Subvox uses **SUBTEST** (Solana devnet) to reward contributors and power the community economy.

- Contributors earn tokens for merged PRs and bug reports
- Users in the community pool get free transcription credits
- Token holders earn a share of platform revenue
- No monetary value — pure community utility

See [rewards/](rewards/) for full tokenomics.
